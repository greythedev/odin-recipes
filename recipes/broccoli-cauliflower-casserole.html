Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 2 cups cauliflower
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 1(15 ounces) package of crushed seasoned croutons

Steps on making broccoli cauliflower casserole

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place broccoli and cauliflower in separate saucepans, cover with water, and bring to a boil; cook until tender, 3 to 4 minutes. Drain and arrange in an 11x7-inch baking dish.
  3. Beat eggs in a bowl until creamy. Stir in 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, and onion; pour over vegetables. Pour melted butter on top and sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese. Scatter crushed croutons evenly on top.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until browned and bubbling, about 40 minutes.
